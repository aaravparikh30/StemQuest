About

Welcome to our Research Opportunities website! We are dedicated to providing a platform where you can explore various research opportunities available at the University of Utah. Created by a student who struggled finding research opportunities in high school, STEMQuest aims to provide access to professors who may be offering research opportunities in their lab for the summer. For more information, see the Available Opportunities tab below.

Why This Website?

At the StemQuest, we believe in the power of research to drive innovation and create a positive impact on society. This website aims to streamline the process of finding and applying for research opportunities, making it easier for high school students and researchers to discover projects aligned with their interests and expertise.

By centralizing the information about research opportunities, we aim to foster collaboration, encourage interdisciplinary research, and create a vibrant research community for high schoolers getting interested in getting involved.

Autonomously closing vents for bicycle helmets based on hydrogel-functionalized meshes

Opportunity to work on a proof-of-principle bicycle helmet with autonomously closing vents (suitable for engineers, material scientists, chemists). Smart hydrogels are materials that able to change their volume depending on environmental parameters like, e.g., temperature and light. Especially temperature and light sensitivity of our specifically designed hydrogels can be exploited to let them perform autonomous sensing and actuating tasks. We plan to use this function for the autonomous closing and opening of vents, e.g., in bicycle helmets or tent screens. The mesh will be coated with smart hydrogels we develop in our lab. In the dry state the hydrogel has its smallest volume and, as a result, the vents are open. In wet conditions, i.e. rain, the hydrogel fibers swell and the vents are autonomously being closed within minutes. When the rain stops, the vents will open again thanks to the mesh's environmental responsivity.
